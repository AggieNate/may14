Yo Gabba Gabba!

This show is downright weird. It's so creative, though, and it encourages kids to actively participate. The guy who created this show also created one of my other favorite shows -- The Aquabats! Super Show!

Nominally “Yo Gabba Gabba!” is a show that uses cuddly creatures — a cat-dragon hybrid, an adorable cyclops, a flower-thingy — and bouncy music to teach little ones, ages 5 and under, that it’s cool to apologize when they make mistakes and to try foods that they haven’t tasted before.

-- DAVE ITZKOFF, New York Times --
